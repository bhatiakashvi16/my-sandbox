HOGWARTS HOUSES



Gryffindor

Gryffindor values bravery, courage, determination, and chivalry. Students of this house are known for standing up for what is right. Its founder was Godric Gryffindor.

Slytherin

Slytherin values ambition, leadership, resourcefulness, and cunning. Students of this house are often determined to achieve their goals. Its founder was Salazar Slytherin.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw values intelligence, wisdom, creativity, and learning. Students of this house enjoy solving problems and gaining knowledge. Its founder was Rowena Ravenclaw.

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff values loyalty, kindness, patience, and hard work. Students of this house are known for being fair and accepting of others. Its founder was Helga Hufflepuff.
